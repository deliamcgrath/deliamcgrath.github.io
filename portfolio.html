Pet Robot

At Konpanion, our pet robot Maah was created to address loneliness among the elderly.

We explore human-robot interaction, cognitive AI, and game agent design. How can robots develop personalities or form memories? I lead Maah’s adaptive behavior and perception features for long-term use.

Breathing Wearable

Draughts Vision Tracker

Vision pipeline to analyze draughts gameplay from video/stills. Steps: HSV classification, motion detection, King piece recognition.

  • Pixel Classification: HSV & back-projection histograms
  • Piece Detection: Transform + center validation (97% accuracy)
  • Video Tracking: GMM frame selection, 84% move accuracy

Migraine Tracker/Predictor

Adaptive model using sensors and voice input to track and predict migraines.
